OUR TECHNOLOGY

What if off road vehicles could see, understand and proactively prepare for the terrain ahead?

Terrain Intelligence

Our self learning, on vehicle software platform uses forward facing sensors cameras, radar or lidar and AI to interpret terrain shape, incline gradient, surface type, obstacles and more.

Off Road OS

Our complete control stack enables vehicles to pre emptively self select driveline, suspension and torque settings before arriving at every obstacle, incline or change in surface.
